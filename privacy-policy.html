Privacy Policy – Homework Solver

Effective Date: May 4, 2025

Homework Solver (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect limited device data (e.g., device ID, OS version) for analytics and abuse prevention.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data on how users interact with the app (e.g., number of solves, ads viewed).
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and other data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Grant credit for rewarded ads
  • Prevent abuse (e.g., reinstallation exploits)
  • Serve relevant advertisements

3. Ads and Monetization

Homework Solver uses Google AdMob to serve rewarded ads. Watching an ad gives you one additional solve. AdMob may collect personal data per Google’s policies.

See Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with any third parties except trusted partners required to operate the app, such as:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Firebase (credit tracking and abuse protection)
  • Render (OpenAI proxy API backend)

5. Your Choices

You can choose whether or not to watch ads. Watching a rewarded ad grants one solve. No personal data is required to use the app.
